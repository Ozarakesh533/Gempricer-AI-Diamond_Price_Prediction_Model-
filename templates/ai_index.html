The 4Cs of Diamonds

Cut

Precision and artistry of the diamond’s shape.
  • Round Brilliant: Classic and highly brilliant.
  • Princess: Square-shaped with modern elegance.
  • Cushion: Soft, rounded vintage style.
  • Emerald: Hall-of-mirrors effect.
  • Oval: Elongated brilliance.
  • Pear: Teardrop-shaped elegance.
  • Marquise: Striking football shape.
  • Heart: Romantic and symbolic.
  • Asscher: Vintage square step-cut.

Clarity

Presence of inclusions or blemishes.
  • FL: Flawless under 10x.
  • IF: Internally Flawless, minor surface blemishes.
  • VVS1/VVS2: Minute inclusions.
  • VS1/VS2: Small inclusions.
  • SI1/SI2: Noticeable under magnification.
  • I1–I3: Visible inclusions.

Carat

Weight that heavily affects value.
  • Higher Carat: Larger, heavier diamond.
  • Value Impact: Key factor in pricing.
  • Appearance: More striking presence.

Color

Natural tint; colorless is most valuable.
  • D–F: Colorless, rare and valuable.
  • G–J: Near colorless, excellent value.
  • K–M: Faint tint.
  • N–Z: Very light to light tints.
  • Fancy Colors: Rare vivid hues.
